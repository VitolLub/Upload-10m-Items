STOCKchckr

In Stock Quantities Checker for local US Stores: Walmart, Target, Toys R Us, Walgreens and CVS.

Gift buyers and online sellers use this store checker to check store availability for hot products. Allows you to see how many units store estimates are in stock at local US stores.
You get to choose where you want to search on a map, so you can search a region or the entire country.
You can add products to your shopping list and then sort by store so you have a buy list for each physical store location.

Install from Chrome Web Store
